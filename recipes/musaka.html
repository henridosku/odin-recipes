Musaka Recipe

Short Description

Introducing Musaka - Albanian Style Recipe, a hearty and delicious comfort
food straight from the heart of Albania. This dish is perfect for those
chilly evenings when you crave something warm and filling.
Our Musaka recipe includes thinly sliced potatoes fried in butter, tender
ground beef simmered with onions, and a delicious blend of spices including
oregano and paprika. The entire dish is then topped with a layer of scalded
milk and well-beaten eggs, creating a creamy and indulgent finish. Did you
know that Musaka is often compared to moussaka, a popular Greek dish? While
both dishes share some similarities, Musaka is a uniquely Albanian creation
that has been enjoyed for generations. To make the perfect Musaka, be sure
to slice your potatoes thinly and fry them to a golden brown before creating
your layers. It's also important to slowly pour the egg-milk mixture over
the casserole to ensure that each layer is well-coated.
Musaka - Albanian Style Recipe is the perfect addition to any meal, and can
even be enjoyed as a snack or appetizer. Try it today and experience the
rich and flavorful taste of Albania.

Ingridients

  • 8 to 10 potatoes, peeled, washed & sliced thin (Fry in butter.)
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 1/2 lb. ground beef
  • 3 onions, simmered
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • salt and pepper
  • 1/2 qt. milk, scalded

Steps

  1. Fry hamburger and mix these ingredients.
  2. Arrange a layer of sliced potatoes on bottom of 15 x 9 x 1 1/2 inch casserole or baking pan.
  3. Top with a layer of hamburger mixture.
  4. Repeat a layer of potatoes and hamburger finishing top with a layer of potatoes.
  5. Mix the scalded milk with well beaten eggs slowly.
  6. Pour this egg-milk mixture over casserole to barely cover top layer.
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
